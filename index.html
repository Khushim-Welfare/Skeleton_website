Our Mission

Khushim Welfare is dedicated to bringing joy, education, and essential resources to underprivileged children across Nepal. We believe that every child, regardless of their circumstances, deserves access to basic necessities, quality education, and the chance to dream big.

Through community partnerships and grassroots initiatives, we work tirelessly to break the cycle of poverty and create lasting positive change in rural and urban communities throughout Nepal.
